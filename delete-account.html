Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their AIO Media account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to:

support@aiodev.work

Please include the email address associated with your account.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted

  • Your account will be permanently disabled.
  • Your saved library items may be removed.
  • Your watch history and playback progress may be removed.
  • You may lose access to account-specific features.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Contact

For questions regarding account deletion, contact:

support@aiodev.work
