AIO Media Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

AIO Media respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

When you create or use an AIO Media account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Library and saved content information
  • Watch history and playback progress
  • Diagnostic and crash information used to improve application stability

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide account access and authentication
  • Synchronize viewing progress and saved content
  • Improve application performance and reliability
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain service security

Third-Party Services

AIO Media may use third-party services to provide application functionality, including:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) for movie and television metadata
  • Apple services required for iOS functionality
  • Cloud hosting providers used to operate our services

These providers may process information as necessary to deliver their services.

Data Sharing

AIO Media does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Information may be shared only when required to:

  • Provide core application functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services

Data Retention

Account information is retained while your account remains active. Information may be removed upon account deletion request, subject to legal or operational requirements.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting:

support@aiodev.work

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

AIO Media is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

support@aiodev.work
